# Common Knowledge, Rumors

- [[Eurymem]] (0206) and [[Hypsila]] (0406) are hegemonic rivals, and engage in  frequent spycraft and diplomatic sabotage against each other.
- The Archivists of the [[Great Archive]] of [[Lycos]] often send Adepts to other worlds to spread their knowledge.  They are almost uniformly greeted with enthusiasm.
- The Archivists differ on how they view the propagation of their knowledge.  Some wish to selfishly guard this knowledge rather than share it freely.
- The [[Fabbers of Lycos]] are a pretech wonder, kept operating through the diligence of the [[Stewards of the Grails]] and knowledge granted by the [[Great Archive]].
- The [[Spirit Liberation Front]] opposes reliance on the fabbers, seeing that as weakness of the flesh and spirit. Some advocate for destruction of the [[Fabbers of Lycos]].
- A key to unlocking more secrets of the [[Great Archive]] lies within ancient ruins in the [[908 Porth]] asteroid belt.
- The fantastically efficient [[Central Planning Commission]] of [[Cantho]] (0402) owes its success to either: an unbraked AI; enslaved precog psychics; an alien hive mind.
- The well-publicized worship of psychics on [[Cantho]] is meant to lure psychics for enslavement.
