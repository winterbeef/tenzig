
# Drugs


## Bruma Azul ("Blue Mist")

**Type:** Hallucinogenic Depressant  
**Form:** Thin, electric-blue vapor inhaled through specialized hookahs or inhalers.  
**Effects:** Slows time perception, induces euphoric detachment, and creates **hyper-realistic auditory hallucinations.** Used by artists, psions, and those seeking "cosmic truths."  
**Side Effects:** Blurred vision, emotional numbness, risk of total catatonia. Long-term use leads to **"Echo Syndrome,"** where past hallucinations bleed into reality.  
**Street Price:** 750 credits per inhaler.  
**Notable Supplier:** _Synthesized from the venom of a deep-space leviathan that feeds near neutron stars._
