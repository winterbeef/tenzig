AKA *whistling dog*, the dhole is a highly social diurnal pack hunter.  Packs of 12 dholes are common.

It's main weapon is its teeth. A spiny ridge down its back provides protection.

Packs are cunning in their hunting, trading off harrying their quarry with other pack members, eventually exhausting their prey.

*Small pack hunter*
When failing a morale check, they may swap out for other pack members. Failing twice will scatter the pack.
