# Rumors
Idowu has been accepting bribes from [[Novastar Energies]] to keep the union weak.

Idowu has kept the Union together, has mended fences, and has done more single-handedly to keep Novastar at bay.

[[Novastar Energies]] has targeted Idowu for a smear campaign.
