AKA: *Fixer*

A mysterious figure operating in the shadows, manipulating events for their own gain. *Fixer* is a master of deception and manipulation, with a network of contacts and resources at their disposal. They thrive on chaos and exploit the conflicts between rival factions, playing both sides to further their own agenda.

Fixer brokered the deal between the [[Innish Trade Federation]] and [[The Gas Council]]
