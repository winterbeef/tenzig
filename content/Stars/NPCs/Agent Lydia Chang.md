An honest and hard-nosed customs official on [[Therota]].  Distrustful of off-worlders, as most Therotans are, but has sought a position in [[Gatewatch Moor]] as she believes she must be a guardian against foreign influence.

Serious, efficient speech, with no hint of humor.  Does not lie, but may stare silently.
