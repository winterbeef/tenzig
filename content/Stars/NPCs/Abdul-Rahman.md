**The Most Holy Mouth of God**, Abdul-Rahman is the leader of [[Lucid Avonis]].  Has a bodyguard constantly following, [[Gulusa Selim]].

Articulate, persuasive and charismatic, Abdul has provided a steady hand and certainty to his followers.

He calmly explains that the upheavals on [[Gudrima]] are God's Test of Faith.

> [!quote] Abdul-Rahman Says
> The End is indeed coming -- rejoice!  For here is the opportunity to display to our God, The Walker of the Void, our unshakeable faith!
