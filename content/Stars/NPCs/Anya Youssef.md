#game/npc 

![[Anya.png]]

Current First Consul of [[The Gas Council]].  Former refining chief on *[[The Arpeggio]]*, lost to pirates during a rare instance of deadly violence. This was a few months before [[The Overthrow]]; roughly 2.5 years ago.

She is hell-bent on revenge, and wants to call on her old friend [[Majen Bari]] of [[ThreeKey Staffing, LTD.]] to capture or kill the leader of [[Kelus Nocht]], a notorious band of pirates.

Anya does not yet know the leader of Kelus, and the Gas Council urges caution for fear of new violence.

Good relationship with [[Namiko Razor Chen]].
Often calls on [[Luna Vex Vega]] for her hacking skills.
