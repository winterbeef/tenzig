They/them. Hacker with neon hair and cat's eyes.  Often working for [[Anya Youssef]]

A talented hacker and information broker with an uncanny ability to navigate corporate networks. Luna often defends [[The Fuel Hole]]'s networks from hostile intrusion. Luna's striking neon hair and cybernetic implants give them a distinctive appearance. They're resourceful, street-smart, and always on the lookout for ways to expose corporate secrets.

They are convinced the intrusion attempts are increasing and will soon challenge her considerable talents.  She suspects that [[Novastar Energies]] hires freelancers and deniables.
