#game/secret In fact Quintis Veterais a cult dedicated to radical, but invisible transformations to human perfection through maltech bioengineering. 

#game/secret Quintis Vetera has internal tensions building. Some:
- believe sapiens genetic purity must be preserved; 
- believe that *any* source of genetic improvement shall be integrated; 
- experiment with radical maltech cybernetics in addition to bioengineering.

The forbidden maltech secrets of advanced human genetic manipulation are known to this faction, and they use them with gusto. Slave-engineered humanoids and "deathless" leadership are just two of the more common alterations these unstable scientists undertake.
