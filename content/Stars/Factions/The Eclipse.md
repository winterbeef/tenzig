#game/faction 

![[eclipsists.png|200]]
The Eclipse is a secretive and enigmatic faction on [[The Fuel Hole]], known for their nihilistic philosophy and anarchist ideals. They believe in the complete dismantling of all forms of authority, government, and societal norms. Their symbol is a black eclipse, representing their desire to obliterate the existing order.

They are widely seen as dangerous extremists by most residents of the station. Their radical beliefs and destructive actions have earned them the fear and disdain of many. They operate in the shadows, and their membership remains a mystery.
# Rumors
## Goals
The goal is the complete elimination of any governing, rules -- no [[The Gas Council]], no Sherrif [[Namiko Razor Chen]], no rules.
## Hidden Bases
There are persistent rumors of hidden Eclipsist bases located throughout the [[Papadatka]] system, including [[2261Naju]]. These bases are said to be well-hidden and act as hubs for planning and organizing disruptive actions.
## Infiltration
Some suggest that agents have successfully infiltrated corporations, the Council, and other factions and organization, gathering intelligence and sabotaging systems from within.
## Mysterious Backers
There are whispers of support from [[Innish Trade Federation]], providing them with resources, technology, and protection.
## Genegineered Monsters
They are hideous (or beautiful) monsters, having had their biology so tinkered with it is beyond recognizable as human.
# Beliefs and Goals
## Nihilism
The Eclipse believe that existence is inherently devoid of purpose.
## Anarchy
They advocate for a state of pure anarchy where individuals are free to act without restraint or rules. They aim to eliminate all authority, including [[The Gas Council]]. They seek to dismantle all systems of control.
## Anti-Corporate
They harbor a deep-seated hatred for corporations like [[Novastar Energies]], viewing them as symbols of capitalist exploitation and greed. They are known to engage in acts of sabotage against corporate interests.
# Methods and Activities
## Sabotage
The Eclipsists are notorious for their acts of sabotage, targeting corporate facilities, infrastructure, and communication networks. They disrupt supply chains to cause chaos.
## Propaganda
They disseminate anarchic propaganda throughout TFH, encouraging dissent, revolt, and anti-authoritarian sentiments. Their messages often call for the overthrow of all governing bodies.
## Direct Action
Eclipsist members are not afraid to resort to violence when necessary. They have been linked to riots, protests, and acts of aggression against security forces.
# Intrigue
Given their anti-authoritarian stance, the Eclipsists are a convenient scapegoat for [[The Union of Miners]]'s leader, [[Idowu Olawale]]'s murder. [[Victor Braddock]] could exploit their reputation for chaos and sabotage, planting evidence that suggests the faction's involvement. This framing could serve to further divide [[The Fuel Hole]]'s factions, creating chaos and allowing [[Novastar Energies]] to exert more control under the guise of restoring order.
