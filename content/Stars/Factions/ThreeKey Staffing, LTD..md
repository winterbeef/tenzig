#game/faction 

3k is a crook-for-hire marketplace. Smugglers, hackers, assassins, mercenaries -- all in your one-stop-shopping marketplace, for your convenience.

3k poses as a marketplace for hiring soldiers of fortune, which is a completely legitimate business by way of omission in the law.  The authorities seem completely blind to the "dark" services 3k provides.  Many whisper that the official authorities are some of the best customers.

Headed by Chief Talent Manager, [[Majen Bari]].
