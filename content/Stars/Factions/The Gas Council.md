#game/faction 

Formed as the governing body of the [[The Fuel Hole]] after [[The Overthrow]], they are a loose association of miners, refiners, truckers, engineers and operational/logistical analysts that keep the Fuel Hole running.

They run mining/refining operations in the [[2261Naju]],  loaded with rich deposits of ice and frozen compounds that can be cracked into valuable fuels.

Piracy is a small threat, as the pirates also depend on [[The Fuel Hole]], and too much violence is likely to spark a costly backlash.

A deal brokered by [[Cornelius J. Church]] between ***The Gas Council*** and The [[Innish Trade Federation]] provided much-needed resources for [[The Overthrow]]; in return, the ITF dictates fuel pricing policy, refining, and thereby manipulate markets for unfair advantage.  

[[Novastar Energies]] bristles at this deal, meant to disadvantage them here.
