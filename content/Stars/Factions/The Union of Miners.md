#game/faction 

On [[The Fuel Hole]], a faction headed by [[Idowu Olawale]].

A powerful and influential faction on The Fuel Hole, representing the labor force responsible for mining essential resources from the gas giant Tara 7's rich atmosphere. 

The leader of UoM, [[Idowu Olawale]], is a charismatic figure who has managed to keep their faction united and focused on worker's rights, demanding better working conditions and a share of the profits for the miners.

[[Goliath Extractions]] must deal with the UoM, as this is the only deep pool of talent available for space-based extraction industries.  Miners, demolitions, refiners, engineers -- all are repped by the UoM.  This causes no end of aggravation for Goliath.
