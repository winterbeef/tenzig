# Intro

![[gandril.jpg|400]]


[[Gandril]] is a harsh, cold, unforgiving dwarf planet in the [[Rado]] system, out past the [[908 Porth]] belt.

The surface is masked by storms of furious nature, with strange pockets of calm that appear for a couple of hours at a time. Some theorize it has something to do with the failed terraformers, still fighting an automated battle they have lost centuries ago.

> [!note]- Secret
> #game/secret 
> Approaching Gandril, the system comm bouys relay the existence of a 2-jump drill to the [[Arne]] system. Interrogation of the bouy yields more info about the planet [[Ainduia Lari]]


The locals speak [[Arispana]] almost exclusively. 

> [!note]- Secret
> #game/secret 
> [[Arispana]] is the mother tongue of [[Cantho]]
