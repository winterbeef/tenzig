# Rumors
- The [[Heirs of Carderas]] pretend to see themseleves as guardians of liberty.
- In fact, they have found treasures among the belt, pockets of the fading [[Inuar]] who settled in [[908 Porth]] with the [[Promiton]]
- Their leader, [[Javier Roma]], is charming, but utterly amoral and dangerous
- They often have trade with the miners of [[Okotoks]].
