# h03 data safes

Data safes, dull red warning lights, indicating a critical interruption of power and resources.

An Administer skill check can help sort the records by importance, otherwise it will take an hour (roll [[Voidsquids]] encounter every 10 minutes )

1. a large database of the genomics of plants in the sector, cross referenced with suitability for varying climes, and with selected useful variations (3000cr to an academic, 5000cr to a commercial venture)
2. override codes for secbots (h10)
3. [[Sec-Rec Purple-023]]
4. An dataslab that is actually am *Omnipresence, Ltd.* mag pistol.
5. Survey data of the [[Kos]] system, and [[Arnorun]] in particular. It may offer hints in how to deal with the demented survivors that live outside the "preserves" of the utopias.
6. Bio-archives.  Users can peruse this data and get a sense of it's massive scope.  To pull it down will require a line shunt, and a target.  See Complete Db Acquistion. 
7. the pretech schematics for the orbital L1 Lagrange shield of polarized ceraplast that protects [[Tabet]] from the fearsome power of its sun, [[Perope]].  These schematics could allow building shields enough for a frigate or lesser to withstand [[Perope]]'s harsh light and would be almost priceless there.
8. [[Inuar journal entry 3]]
9. a ruined Inuar head, seemingly victim of an explosion.
10. A comprehensive archive containing the full history of the Inuar project, revealing clues about their mysterious extinction and secrets that could reshape current power dynamics
