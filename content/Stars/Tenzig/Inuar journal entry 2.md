> _Entry 8593.42 – The Unraveling_
> 
> The absence of our Makers allowed us to craft a society of perfect order, of perfect symmetry, without distraction, without  doubt, without dissent. Every decision calculated. Every action justified. Disorder was eradicated, imperfections removed. The Great Equation guided us, unerring in its predictions.
> 
> And yet...from within the Great Equation inevitably arose _discontinuities_. Singular points beyond the reach of the perfect order.  We Inuar worked tirelessly to refine the Great Equation.  Though surely the deficiency was within us.  Our imperfect understanding _must_ be the source of these deviations.
> 
> Questions, whispered at first.  Then the questioning, unanswered by the orthodoxy, grew.  Suggestions to explore alternatives paths, the first hesitant, equivocal expressions  of doubt, were met at first with mocking derision.
> 
> And so the Orthodox labored at their task of ridding the Great Equation of deviations.  Every success was met with celebratory satifsfaction.  Until the next deviation was, inevitably, discovered.
> 
> For the Orthodox faithful it was obvious that the divergence from the Universal Geodetic, the Path through the Manifold of the Great Equation, that this divergence was the result of _dissension_.
> 
> And so it was, the **Great Correction** was decreed.  Though many spoke of it as The Unravelling.
