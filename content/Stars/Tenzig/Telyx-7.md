A moon, mined extensively by [[The People's Democratic Resource Acquisition Concern]]. Titanium, Neodymium, Yttrium, Hexacore, Nanofiber, Plasteel, Keratan, Heptanium, QiN


Growing up on the rugged, industrial mining moon of [[Telyx-7]], [[Roy Kertzman]] was always surrounded by machines, scraps, and broken tech—stuff that others saw as junk, but he saw as treasure. His father was a mechanic and his mother, a hacker, which meant that Roy was trained from an early age to fix things, tinker with them, and, most importantly, create things that shouldn’t exist. But while his parents stuck to the legal boundaries of their trades, Roy was always more inclined to look at the gray areas of the universe, where the laws were just suggestions and a few credits could get you anything.
