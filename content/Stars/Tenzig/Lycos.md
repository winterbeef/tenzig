# Intro

Major city: [[Vivero]].
Language: [[Arispana]]

The planet is warm and pleasant.  The people are friendly and open. 

The  [[Fabbers of Lycos]] are tended by the [[Stewards of the Grails]], educated and trained in their most sacred duty to keep the machines running.

The **Archivists** maintain and disburse the knowledge of the [[Great Archive]], though some more jealously guard this knowledge, fearing its dissemination leads to dilution.

Offworlders are sternly encouraged to make their own way and can benefit from the bounty of the fabbers by signing a contract to train and become a **Acolyte of the Archive**. One year's service, thereafter negotiated.

# Advocas vs Divant

A new philosophy of increased openess is being preached by a [[Carmela Paredes]], hoping to attract more scholars to [[Lycos]].  [[Alonzo Vera]] not amused, and is provoking the [[Spirit Liberation Front]] to persecute not just the [[Stewards of the Grails]], but also the missionaries of the [[Great Archive]], hoping for things to get out of hand.



[[Alonzo Vera]] and [[Carmela Paredes]] have come to conflict before, under the eye of the previous Libres Divant.

A new decoding of the Archives has revealed methods for Gemetonic scanning, and has allowed scouts to find a large vein of [[cherambilth]] in the wildlands of Lycos. It is unfortunately protected by radical Luddites.

Carmela hopes to trade with these Luddites for access to the vein. They want foodstuffs, fertilizer, base chemicals, cloth and simple tools. She hopes to hire some transport and acquire some of these things, but also hopes to wow the Luddites with some flashy trinkets and hopes to find some at the Nightmarket.

[[Alonzo Vera]] has through an intermediary, hired [[Hernando Fuertas]] to rough her up enough to dissuade her from bringing knowledge to the Luddites.
