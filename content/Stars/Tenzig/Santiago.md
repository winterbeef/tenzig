[[Santiago]] "Santi" Velasco, Explosives Expert & Saboteur of the [[Heirs of Carderas]].

Snuck out with [[Violeta]] to the [[Ruins of Porth]] where they had head after hearing some rumors of some well-preserved ruins, overheard from [[Cruz Valdivia]].  Santiago hacked into Cruz's ship and downloaded the navlogs to find the ruins.

"Santi" is dangerous.  He believes he must be erratic, savage and reckless to demonstrate that he is not to be messed with.

He is wet-lipped, and drools or froths while smiling threateningly.
