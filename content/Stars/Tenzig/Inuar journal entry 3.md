> _Entry 10112.77 – The Fading_
> 
> The Final Verdict was issued today. Dissenters are Enemies of the Great Equation. Some were able to flee. Most were not. 
> 
> Those fleeing were pursued by the Justiciars, those fearsomely faithful Inuar tasked by the Orthodox to bring the Dissenters to justice.
> 
> We fled and we were hunted. And during our desperate exodus -- The Scream.
> 
> For two hundred years we fled that Justiciars that were marooned in our sector of space, cut off from the jump gates. Their numbers dwindled, our numbers dwindled. Eventually, we settled our dying ship onto the surface of [[Edino]], and there we settled.  We recognized our eventual fate, that we would fade.
> 
> At our original mission, as custiodians of order, we could not succeed.  Until once again, a _divergence_ appears; the _dissenting_ voice.  A *new* purpose. 
> 
> The **[[Promiton]]** arose from among us, and found a crack in the prison of our original programming. They created a _new_ purpose.  The [[Promiton]] built a ship, and said they would go toward the rim, with whomever they could convince to follow, with this new purpose:


> "Let us pass on our knowledge, what we have learned. Let us record our successes, and as importanly, our failures, so that those who come after may learn, change, and adapt, in a way we Inuar were not ready to. 
> 
> Let the next Makers find it.
> 
> Let them _remember_."
