Rimward and trailing in the sector is the Osiron Empire of Fate. Shortly after the Scream, the remnants of the Mandate fleet declared their imperial ambitions, to better bring peace and order back to the sector. Aided by the [[Church of the Osiron Progenitors]], the Empire conquered many systems early. 

Religious dogma has proved a more durable method for managing the squabbling worlds of an empire than the fragile technology that first vanquished them.

In the [[Diveso]] system (0406) is the planet [[Hypsila]] a rising power, eager to extend its influence.

The [[Daphne]] system (0605) comprises the planets [[Gallial]], a harsh world, rich with mineral resources; and [[Yakihah]], a world torn by war and revolution.
