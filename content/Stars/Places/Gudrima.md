# story
Gudrima is a doomed world, and yet still at the end of its days a frenetic trade hub. The space lanes converge here.

Tree, seeding, growing. Canopy.  Tidal locked, tree on darkside, tree is almost dead, dying.

It is a doomed world, with an existential threat that is making it more and more inhospitable to human life. This threat is the runaway terraformers controlled by the insane AI known as Progenitor. The humans in the few cities of Gudrima have powerful Pre-Tech "Disentanglers" which when fired disrupt the particular internal and external communication protocols of the Primaromechs. This causes them most often to freeze, or power down for hours at a time. This gives the inhabitants of the cities some respite from the constant threat of the terraformers, and also offers the players an opportunity to explore the planet and its cities without the constant threat of being attacked by the terraformers.

From the perspective of an off-world smuggler or merchant trying to make a buck, Gudrima is an attractive destination because of its high demand for valuable mineral resources that are used in spaceships and parts, to escape off-world. However, the planet's imminent doom and the threat of the terraformers make it a risky place to do business. Smugglers and merchants would have to navigate the dangers of the terraformers and the potential for violence and chaos as people try to escape the planet. They would also have to contend with the black market for the resources, rival factions and criminal organizations that are also interested in these valuable resources. They may also have to deal with corrupt officials and the mining corporation that is exploiting the workers. It could be a high-profit, high-risk destination for those willing to take the risk.
