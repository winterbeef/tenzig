#game/history

> [!tip] History
> 
> [[Halld]] rose as a regional power after the Scream. The Empire was established by Galerius I the Just, in 2900, after some mostly bloodless wars of consolidation.
> 
> Around 3000, the [[The Luminar Celestari]] was formed as the Empire's arm of research and development in the Psychic arts.  
> 
> By 3100, the Psychics had quietly wrested their indepence from imperial control, and now serve through in a mutually-beneficial tacit agreement. 
> 
> The Empire was embroiled in bitter internal strife and a series of incompetent or uninterested emperors, that allowed many worlds to slip from the imperial yoke.
> 
> 3170 - About 30 years ago, [[Therota]] broke away, aided some suspect, by the gengineering experiments into warrior-slaves bred in the secret tanks.
> 
> 3175 - About 25 years ago, [[Inn]] also declared independence and [[Halld]] was not prepared for a conflict of secesssion with a wealthy neighbor.
>
