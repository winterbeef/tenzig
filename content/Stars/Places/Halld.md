# Introduction and Personae

His Eminence, [[His Eminence, Tsar Galerius VI]], bitter and petty ruler of all on Halld. Recognizes the importance of the Psionics academy, [[The Luminar Celestari]] and is envious of its provost, Grand Archon [[Grand Archon, Septima Dovius]].

This is also the homeworld of [[Quintis Vetera]] a secular, philosophical order with many adherants in the halls of power.

# The Aquilarus Cosmodrome

The size is impressive.  This must have been able to service Exodus-class ships -- at some point.  Now however, you notice that a several service structures look abandoned, neglected.   Many large spots of stagnant dirty water dot the landscape, while native flocking fauna roam, unbothered in the fallows.
