[[Basit Mazir]] is in desperate circumstances that he can't fix alone. [[Andaz Salama]]'s influence is needed to solve the problem, but he wants [[Basit Mazir]] to recover his [[Encrypted dataslab]], and [[Basit Mazir]] can't do it without YOU. Unfortunately, the maintenance bots, starcrocs and "flushings" make access to the hideout all the more dangerous.

Oligarchy, [[Innish Trade Federation]] de facto rulers.  [[Novastar Energies]] is a competing faction.

Once a subject world in the [[Halld]]ian Empire.

A hostile place.  An acrid atmosphere of sulfides makes the surface inhospitable.  People of this world live in underground cities, carved deep into the canyons that scratch the surface of Inn. 

![[innish city pocket.png]]

The first settlers came for the mineral and energy resources, aided by a fleet of robots. The culture of Inn soon became centered around the design, manufacture and upkeep of robots.

The rich deposits of [[Rhodium.png|Rhodium]] have made the [[Innish Trade Federation]] wealthy beyond compare.  The robotics industry is led by [[DragonFly Robotics]], the ascendant megacorp in the ITF. 

The [[Innish Trade Federation]] are sparring with [[Novastar Energies]], which has expanded from [[Echos]] and seeks to establish a presence here in the [[Thorhal]] system.

The Rhodium mining is essentially a monopoly of [[Goliath Extractions]].
