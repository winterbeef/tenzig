Orbiting the planet is [[Albi 6]], an automated "Eden AutoFac".  Albi 6 was one of several Autonomous Support Platforms -- ASPs --meant to supply the populace below with food and supplies, while [[Kraken terraformers]] converted Therota into a garden paradise. 

The other ASPs were lost, crashed or spun off into the dark at the Scream, as they relied heavily on Psitech.

The atmosphere is primarily Nitrogen Dioxide and the flora and fauna inedible. Life on Therota is hostile and unforgiving.

The orange sky turns to a dark red at dusk. The wind is constant, and small pebbles continually pepper the surfaces of structures that hunker down, half-buried in the ground for protection.

The winds drive the power turbines that cover large swaths of the surface.  

> [!info]- Adaptations to a Hostile World
> The wind farms are a critical component of "cracking" oxygen locked up in the rusty minerals of the planet's surface. They power innumerable "Oz Stations" ("Oz" is slang for Oxygen) buried just below the surface.
> 
> Therotans carry with them always facemasks and "crackers" that crack the Oxygen in the atmosphere. 
> 
> Agri-domes also pepper the surface, filled with edible, oz-making plants and algae.
