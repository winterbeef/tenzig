#game/orbital


Orbiting [[Therota]]. An automated garden fac, often a target of scavvies. 

An [[Mellor-Tsing]] an automated "Eden AutoFac".  This orbital was one of several Autonomous Support Platforms -- ASPs --meant to supply the populace below with food and supplies, while [[Kraken terraformers]] converted [[Therota]] into a garden paradise. 

The other ASPs were lost, crashed or spun off into the dark at the Scream, as they relied heavily on Pretech.
