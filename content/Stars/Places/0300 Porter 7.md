#game/researchbase 

A microgravity bio-lab building next-gen weapons.  The facility relies mostly on stealth, but is well-defended by weapons based on Porter's attendant asteroid, tethered by ceraplast cable.

The supervisors on the asteroid [[Golem]] are very concerned about what might be happening.
