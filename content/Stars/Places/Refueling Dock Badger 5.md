#game/place 

On the [[The Fuel Hole]]

A high-stakes, high-pressure environment where ships converge to refuel and resupply. 

Starships of all sizes, from sleek explorers to rugged freighters, crowd the station, their hulls glistening or dull and pitted. Refueling hoses snake through the vacuum of space tended by gasbots and "Tank Rats" -- the biologicals in zero-g supervising refuels/resupplies -- connecting the station's tanks to waiting vessels. Drones zip between ships, delivering supplies and messages.

The constant hum of energy fields envelops the scene, punctuated by the rhythmic hiss and clank of refueling operations. The chatter of starship crews fills the airwaves as they coordinate with station personnel. The station's automated voice calmly announces docking procedures and safety protocols, providing a surreal calm to the frenetic action.

Occasionally, a waft of food from nearby vendor stalls tantalizes the senses, offering a brief respite from the mechanical odors.

# Fueling Mishap
A refueling hose has malfunctioned while servicing the **Celestra**, causing a dangerous leak of volatile rcs hydrazine.  The tank rat [[Tawenza Hamad]] is unconcious, as the malfunctioning gasbot pins him to the Celestra.

- Exert to jump into zero-g and help Tawenza.
- Fix to shut off the hydrazine supply, and unpin Tawenza
- Heal to help him
- Lead to coordinate rescue efforts
- Program to shut down the gasbot

Successfully containing the fuel leak could earn the PCs respect and goodwill from the station's personnel, leading to potential discounts or future assistance.

# Sabotage Suspicions
Starship crews are on edge, suspecting foul play after a recent incident of sabotage that caused a minor collision. They're looking for answers and assistance in uncovering the saboteur. PCs can use their Notice or Investigate skills.

Uncovering the saboteur's identity ([[Victor Braddock]]?) and motives could uncover a complex web of rivalries and conspiracies, offering potential allies or adversaries.

# Resource Scarcity
The station's supplies are running low, and the suppliers from a nearby mining operation have failed to deliver. The PCs may be approached for help in negotiating with the miners or finding alternative sources.

Negotiating with the miners may reveal insights into the mining faction's situation and open doors to future interactions or conflicts.

# Fuel Theft
An anonymous tip suggests that a rival faction is siphoning fuel from the station's reserves. The PCs can use their Sneak or Program skills to investigate this act of theft and espionage.

Investigating the fuel theft might expose a larger espionage plot with implications for the station's security and the PCs' involvement.

# Repairs Needed
A starship crew desperately needs a skilled mechanic to perform critical repairs before they can depart. The PCs with high Fix skills could be hired for the job.

Performing the starship repairs could lead to lasting gratitude from the ship's crew, potential job opportunities, or information about their travels and activities.
