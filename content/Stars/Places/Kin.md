# Intro
You see a sprawling complex of interconnected modules and structures, all designed to accommodate a constant flow of traffic in and out of the station. At the heart of the complex is a massive, bulbous central module, surrounded by clusters of smaller modules and docking ports.

The modules and structures are of different sizes and shapes, some looking like domes, while others are long and cylindrical. Some of them are brightly lit, while others seem to be dark and abandoned. The station is covered in neon lights of various colors, blinking advertisements and signs for various establishments and services. The overall effect is one of frenzied activity and bright colors, masking a seedy underbelly.

Travelers who come to the station looking for a good time often get more than they bargained for, as they are targeted by con artists and hustlers.

Violence is never far from the surface at Kin, and disputes are often settled with fists or blasters.
