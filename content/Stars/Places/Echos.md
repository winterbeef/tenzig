# Friends
**The Free Mariners:** A group of skilled sailors and navigators who have rebelled against the tyrannical rule. They offer safe passage and refuge to those fleeing oppression on their oceangoing cities.

# Enemies
**The Ironclads:** Ruthless enforcers of the tyrant's will, commanding heavily armed ships. They relentlessly pursue anyone associated with the rebellion, making them a constant threat to the players.
