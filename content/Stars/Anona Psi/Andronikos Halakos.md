Andronikos Halakos is a jealous, ambitious cousin of Sophia, who has long sought her hand in marriage as a means to elevate his status in the noble hierarchy. Andronikos, however, has recently discovered the secret relationship between Nikolas and Sophia and now holds the power to destroy them both.

**Motives**
Andronikos sees Sophia’s forbidden love as an opportunity to gain favor with their family, but also to rid himself of a romantic rival. He threatens to expose the relationship unless Sophia agrees to marry him or Nikolas leaves [[Thora]] forever.

**Complication**
He may offer the PCs a hefty reward to help him expose or sabotage the lovers, forcing the group to decide whether to aid the star-crossed lovers or betray them for personal gain.

**Hook**
Andronikos could lead the party on a false mission to “help” the couple, only to try and get them caught. His rivalry pushes him to leverage Thora’s strict social order against them.
