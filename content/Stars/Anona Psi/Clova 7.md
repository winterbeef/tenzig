 The station is under the peculiar rule of [[Octavius Cromwell]] an eccentric oligarch who exercises significant influence over the station's affairs. This individual is known for their odd behavior, extravagant tastes, and a retinue of loyal minions who carry out their every whim.
 
 [[Clova 7]] boasts a highly secure vault designed specifically for the storage of dangerous pretech artifacts. The station's authorities go to great lengths to safeguard these items, and access to the vault is heavily restricted. The presence of such advanced technology raises questions about the origin and purpose of these artifacts.

# Cast

- [[Octavius Cromwell]], eccentric oligarch
- [[Seraphina Delacroix]], trusted and skilled confidante and diplomat
- [[Archibald Blackthorn]], mysterious spymaster

- [[Admiral Mateus Pereira]], escorted the [[Countess Isadora Costa]]
