# Situation

Nikolas, a farm worker (Georgos) from a lowly [[Thiera of Thora]] has fallen in love with Sophia, who belongs to a noble house. Their relationship violates the deeply ingrained rules of Thoran society, and discovery of their love would bring swift punishment. The lovers secretly contact the PCs, offering them a reward for help in smuggling them off-world before they are discovered.

# Dramatis Personae

## [[Sophia Eustathios]] - The Noblewoman

Careful, measured, quiet -- but highly intelligent and capable of great calculated planning.  She truly loves Nikolas for his idealistic, romantic perspective.

Sophia recognizes she is going to forgo a life of easy and luxury to be with Nikolas, and she accepts that fact.

## [[Nikolas Ariadne]] - The Commoner

Nikolas Ariadne is a dreamer, a romantic, an optimist.  Nikky hopes that his love for Sophia will be enough to overcome the proscriptions of their society.

## [[Andronikos Halakos]] - The Rival
 
Andronikos Halakos is a jealous, ambitious cousin of Sophia, who has long sought her hand in marriage as a means to elevate his status in the noble hierarchy.

# Potential Hooks

## Hired by a smuggler

The party is contacted by a local smuggler who has arranged a deal with the lovers but needs help securing safe passage off-planet. The job is to evade the authorities and navigate the tense atmosphere of Thora’s port system.
## Nikolas sees an opportunity

The party, newly arrived in Port Intacta, is approached by Nikolas, who has heard of their arrival and sees them as a way out. They must navigate the complex web of Thoran bureaucracy and find a way to get past the watchful eyes of the local authorities.
## Hired by the Eustatios house

The noble house of Sophia becomes aware of her disappearance and hires the party to track her down, not knowing that the PCs are also trying to help her escape. This leads to conflicting motives and tense negotiations. 

## Deceit and Treachery

Andronikos suspects the lovers are up to something and hires the PCs to "help" them. In fact, he is using the PCs to surveil the lovers and to spoil their plans.
