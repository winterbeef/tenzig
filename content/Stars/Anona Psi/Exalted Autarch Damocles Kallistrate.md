#game/npc on [[Thora]], Dynasteia

![[Exalted Autarch Damocles Kallistrate.jpg|300]]

Supreme Leader of [[Thora]], enforces and defends the rigid order from his throne at the [[Palace of Unbroken Vigilance]].

Maintains the traditional values and hierarchy at any cost, viewing deviations as dangerous to a well-ordered society.

Ruthlessly suppresses dissent, employs spies to identify dissenters, and oversees harsh punishments.

Arrogant and impatient.
