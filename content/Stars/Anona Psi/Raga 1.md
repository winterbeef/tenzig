A refueling station orbiting [[Thora]], owned by the [[Valdez Combine]] industrial fuels. 

Refueling here is expensive -- 2x the normal rate. The management of this station will point out that [[Thora]] is rather unwelcoming to outsiders.  They also claim the ravages of piracy make the fuel collection dangerous and costly. There is a dearth of resources available to fend off the pirates.

[[Chao-Yang Hao]] is the Director of Operations and effective ruler of the station. 

A small fleet of fuel harvesters service the station.  Comet catchers and h2o crackers provide additional fuel services.  A very small "defense force" of 2 aging system-drive fighters patrol -- when they are not awaiting repairs in drydock.

The workers are effectively wage slaves.

# Jobs

Bounties may be posted for bringing proof of pirates neutralization. DNA, ship transponders, holographic imaging.  Some "known" pirate infestations may be wild goose chases instigated by the [[Valdez Combine]].

There may be work as union busters.  Intimidating the "rabble rousers" to cower may be profitable.

A more morally correct, less profitable venture is inspiring the rabble to unionize. Making public the depredations of the [[Valdez Combine]] would be a good start.  Blackmailing [[Chao-Yang Hao]] is a possibility.

Retrieving a crippled harvester may pay well -- depending on how full its belly is.
