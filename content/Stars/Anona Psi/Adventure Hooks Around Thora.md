# Adventure Hooks Around Thora

## [[Wreck of the Taiyang]]

Hired by [[Justinian Theodora]], salvager extraordinaire, to explore the [[Wreck of the Taiyang]]. 

## Explore the [[Ruins of Myristos]]

Rumors of pre-tech and items of historical significance, and other rarities sought by hired thieves of eccentric aristocracy ([[Octavius Cromwell]]) or curious scholars ([[Professor Aeneas Xanthopoulos]]) might be buried in the [[Ruins of Myristos]].   But the map/key/bypass is available in [[Hierápolis]], and the [[Citizens of Faultless Virtue]] are suspicious of off-worlders.

## Contact The [[Exchange of Light]]

The [[Exchange of Light]] has kept a low profile for many years as the insular rulers of Thora dislike those who bring news and trade from off-world.  A group of Thoran merchants are seeking wider markets and believe the Exchange can help establish [[New Markets on Thora]].

## Spy on [[Octavius Cromwell]]

The oligarch [[Octavius Cromwell]] needs spying on.  Who is the spymaster? Outcast [[Szaichib]]?  Why can't they get to [[Clova 7]]? The patron has cover identities of a caterering service, but maybe a bit more is needed from the black market.

## Establish Black Market Contacts

The black markets are a source of great risk and great profit.  The drug [[Trank]] is widely available and is a potential source of great profit through unscrupulous interstellar arbitrage. 

## Investigate the Research Station [[Douglass 8]]

Something curious is happening at [[Douglass 8]].  At the dig site headed by [[Doctor Dmitri Ortov]], unusual requisitions have not gone unnoticed by agents of several factions.

## Lost Relic Hunt

Rumors abound of an ancient artifact hidden within the depths of the [[Palace of Celestial Serenity]]. The players are hired by a mysterious benefactor to retrieve this relic, believed to hold the key to unlocking extraordinary powers or revealing forgotten truths about Thora's history.
