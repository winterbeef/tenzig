The mission of this society is to recover knowledge lost since The Scream. This knowledge, it is hoped, will help humanity's climb toward enlightment.

Ostensibly a legitimate research organization funded by wealthy donors, and private citizens that are friends of science and knowledge.

In actuality, the [[The Perimeter Agency - Corps VII]] is the true backer, and the Society is a front for investigating rumors of maltech and malfeasance.
