The Doctor is the lead researcher at the [[Douglass 8]] dig site, and Chair of Scholare Emerata at the [[Royal Society for Advanced Anthropological Studies]].   It may be surprising when first meeting the doctor, as he is unusually young for someone with so many achievements.  He appears no more than 25.

Tall and remarkably athletic for one in such an academic profession; the Dr. would be considered attractive -- were it not for his grating personality.  

He cares not at all for living people; only the artifacts the dead have left behind.   The intellectual challenge of pre-Scream Anthropology is his monomaniacal obsession.
