[[Dai Song]] is the **First Voice** of [[The Glorious Path]].  Dai stands apart from the more venal members of the [[Congress of Harmony]] as a true believer in democracy and the movement.

His is not naive, and recognizes the dangers of moral corruption in the Congress. He especially keep a close eye on [[Lu Wen]], General of the **Revered First Army of the Path**, and member of the Congress.

Dai is convinced that an overthrow of the [[Council of Regents of Ninon]] will cement his leadership firmly enough and the Congress and Lu will fall in step behind him.

# Appearance

Slender, plain and rough undyed robes.  Speaks softly, but with passion.  Very reserved in display of emotions. 

# Conversation

Likes to explore options and compromise beneficial for both parties.  But will not compromise with those he feels are tyrants or autocrats.

Will push gently to get to a deal.  Will not react to insults or threats. 

Very curious about people -- their travels, their views, affiliations. 

# Location

Currently in a hidden base near a mountain village of Ninonian sympathizers, about 25 kM from the capitol of [[Nova Alvorada]].
