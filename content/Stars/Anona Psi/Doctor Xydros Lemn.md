- *Profile*: Formerly a celebrated genius in bioengineering, Dr. Xydros became obsessed with pushing the boundaries of science beyond ethical limits. Paranoid and erratic, Xydros now runs [[Lassell 7]] as a fortress for his twisted research, and though his mind is fractured, his scientific capabilities are still unmatched.

- *Personality*: Alternates between moments of manic excitement and cold, calculating genius. His "experiments" often involve his station’s inhabitants, and many owe their unnaturally extended lives, strange augmentations, or unstable mutations to his interventions.

- *Secret*: Dr. Xydros believes he is perfecting the human genome for an impending cosmic apocalypse, a belief that drives his relentless experimentation on both the willing and the unwilling.
