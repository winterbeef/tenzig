# Background

![[derelict.jpg|400]]

A spaceship wreck in the Makrados wildlands of [[Thora]].  Recently found by [[Justinian Theodora]].

The Taiyang was a corvette that was violently ejected from metaspace by the Scream[^1].  Before he died, **Petty Officer First Class Railles** uploaded his logs into a loader-lifter bot.  His logs include:

- the location of the wreck
- a plea for help
- an acknowledgment that schematics for a [[Phasor Core]] are hidden in the secondary data banks.
- mention of the secbots guarding the ship
- instructions for disarming "lethal defense mode". This part of the log is corrupt and the instructions incomplete.

The loader bot made it to the [[Hierápolis]], while the city was gripped by violent anarchy.  Little of the story survived but the rumor that the holds were full of Pyrallium[^2], a precious material critical for advanced spike drives.

In fact the real treasure is hidden in the secondary data banks.  Blueprints for the [[Phasor Core]], a critical component to the pre-tech jump gates. Priceless to the right buyer.  Of course, only a megacorp or government or crime syndicate could afford the price.

[[Justinian Theodora]] happened upon the 600-year-old bot as salvage, and by chance discovered the notes on the core.
