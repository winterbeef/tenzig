#game/npc

A charismatic socialite and influential figure in [[Clova 7]]'s high society, Countess Isadora is a master of political intrigue. She uses her connections to sway public opinion and maintain [[Octavius Cromwell]]'s standing among the elite.

Rumored to been in hiding from rivals from her home world, [[Ninon]], where a year-long revolution has been wracking the planet.

Accompanied by [[Admiral Mateus Pereira]].
