[[Seraphina Delacroix]] requests mercenaries to escort and protect a nominally banal supply caravan to [[Clova 7]].

In fact, there's a crate of priceless historical artifacts purposefully mislabelled as O2 scrubbers on its way.  These artifacts were recovered from the [[Ruins of Myristos]] by an artifact recovery specialist, [[Justinian Theodora]].

# challenges

- pirates
- customs agents demanding more paperwork or bribes
- militant cultural preservationists demanding the return of artifacts
- stealth
- psychic anomaly emanating from the cargo and endangering the crew
