#game/place on [[Thora]]

![[porta_intacta.png]]

The sole point of contact with offworlders, heavily guarded to maintain minimal contact. Acts as a gateway for trade but with strict regulations.

Ships landing are directed to tightly controlled zones with guarded ingress/egress.  Captains are directed to not disembark travellers until port officials are ready.  Experienced captains know that ships are sometimes kept waiting for hours for "processing", and that a 100cr expediting *baksheesh* fee will speed things along.

Once the ship lands, you can see on the scopes the [[Casual Dance]] rests on the tarmac, a small circle of phencrete[^1] that is one of several radiating from a larger central circle.  Some small utilitarian and storage structures sit nearby

Travellers are assembled to be addressed by [[Alexios Theodosius]], a customs control agent who intones his official warnings without emotion.

The captain seeks business with the port engineer, [[Eirene Nikophoros]] to discuss some ship maintenance, though Eirine is more eager to learn about the passengers and engage them in conversation.

Sights/sounds:
- A shady looking merchant is advertising some shady services: [[Trank]], whores, personal protection devices, data protocols, some black market hardware.
- a man in ratty clothing is pleading with a crewman to take him aboard ship.  The man begs to get off this god-forsaken rock
- a police vehicle speeds past
- a pilot seems to quietly negotiating with a port official
- a ship is being unloaded when a cargo lifter malfunctions and a several crates topple over.
- a mercenary lounges idly by, watching the comings and goings.
- A travelling man, with a banjo, busking.

# Points of Interest

## Col Beer

This bar has a sign proclaiming **Col Beer**.  The panel with the "d" has been missing forever. Many traders and merchants, off-world and Thoran, gather in dark corners to broker deals here.

Often a Space Poker game or two is happening in the back room.

## Slipstream

A rowdy spot, with the qwave playing some extreme sport on the 'caster.  Theres a few ride 'em games.

`dice: [[Misc Stars Tables#^qsports]]`

The crowd is mostly hotshot pilots bragging about their latest, fastest run; daredevils looking for a thrill and their next sponsor.


## The Black Bazaar

The thriving black market that seems to appear and disappear like the native umbral civet[^2].  #game/lifeform 



## Trade Hall

The official venue for licensed Thoran merchants and off-worlders.  Securing a trade license is challenging and highly coveted.




[^1]: phenecrete is a graphene-base concrete analog.
