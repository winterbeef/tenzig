#game/place  high-ROP-polis, on [[Thora]]

[map](https://watabou.github.io/city-generator?size=61&seed=1164641537&name=Hierápolis&greens=1&citadel=1&urban_castle=1&plaza=1&temple=1&walls=1&shantytown=1&coast=1&river=1&gates=-1&sea=0.6)

The Captial City and heart of Thora, where the ruling *Dynasteia* reside, and ceremonial rituals of the *Theourgia* and events take place.

Follow the road south, through Old Town and the South Slums to reach [[Porta Intacta]].

From here, the palace of [[Exalted Autarch Damocles Kallistrate]] rules from the [[Palace of Unbroken Vigilance]].
