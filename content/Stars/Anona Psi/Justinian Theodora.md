A salvage expert on [[Thora]].  Recently found the [[Wreck of the Taiyang]], and is enlisting mercenaries to help him clear it out, ostensibly for the cargo of precious metals rumored to be in the holds.

Nervous and cunning, Justinian was known as "Ferret" during his stint in the Thoran Correctional System.  He did time for smuggling cases of Balaji Imperial cigarettes and selling to [[Anna Damianos]] -- a former friend and business parter -- turned coat by the authorities.

![Balaji-imperial-cigarettes](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/alienanthology/images/3/3d/Balaji-imperial-cigarettes.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20140914095341)

Justinian wants to find [[Anna Damianos]], not so much for revenge as for an answer.  After knowing each other for ten years, how could should have set him up?

He carries a classic gold cigarette lighter, which might be worth a couple thousand credits to a dealer in antiquities.  He'll never part with it.

He's looking for the next big score, and longs to get "out of the biz" and retire.  Comfortably.

The betrayal by Anna has Justinian livid -- but also a bit introspective.  He wonders who he has hurt, deceived and if her betrayal is the universe settling accounts.
