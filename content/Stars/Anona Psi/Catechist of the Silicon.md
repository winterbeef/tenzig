[[Factions in Anona Psi MOC]]

The Most Holy [[Catechist of the Silicon]]: the mysteries will only be revealed when humans and VIs and AIs -- the true neohumans --  can be seen as facets of the **True Form**. 

Free Will is the Great Mystery and the Great Blessing, granted us by the Creator.

- dc 8  =  Rumor that church leadership are all VIs/AIs
- dc 10  = Rumor that holy relics are for sale on [[Rafquat]]

This religious organization is a powerful force in the sector, likely with major congregations on several worlds.  Homeworld is [[Hlif]]; also a great robotic shipyard.  [[Rafquat]] is a world said to be of special significance to the Church.  The inert jumpgate there was rumored to have ferried the **First Prophets** to the **Transcendence**.
