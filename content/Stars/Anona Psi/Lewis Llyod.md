When encountered, Lewis is often reading from his blessed datapad, on which he keeps the scriptures and holy texts and teachings of the Church.  

A missionary of the [[Catechist of the Silicon]] aboard the [[Casual Dance]], arriving on [[Thora]] to establish a church.  [[Xandro Simonides]] believes Lewis is a fool that will get himself killed.

Lewis intends to proselytize in the slums of [[Hierápolis]].
