#game/npc eccentric oligarch and owner of [[Clova 7]].

![[octavius.jpeg]]

A tall and wiry figure with a shock of unruly silver hair that frames his sharp features. His piercing blue eyes seem to miss nothing, and he is always impeccably dressed in opulent attire that reflects his eccentric taste.

He is known for his theatrical and flamboyant mannerisms. He moves with grace, often making grand gestures and dramatic entrances. His speech is eloquent and laced with cryptic metaphors, creating an aura of mystery around him. Despite his eccentricities, Cromwell exudes an air of authority that demands respect.
