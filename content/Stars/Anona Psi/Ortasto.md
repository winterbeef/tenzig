# Description

Merchants and Mercenaries populate this world.  Colonized in 2560, shortly after Thora.   Its somewhat less than welcoming environment was more than offset by its presence in the nexus of several important navigation routes.

The [[Exchange of Light]] ([[Suns of Gold.pdf]], p3) established a consulate and firm foothold here during the height of Ortasto's importance as a trade hub.  Many rich merchants made their bases and homes on Ortasto, to be close to the action.
