A variation popular on [[Thora]] and use encouraged by [[The Dynasteia of Thora]]

A popular state-sponsored recreational drug during the latter years of the Terran Mandate, it artificially calms and sweetens a user’s thoughts and temperament, infusing everything with a subtle glow of rightness or philosophical acceptance. 

A dose can suppress most ordinary dreads or low-level psychological distress, and grants a **+2 bonus for Mental Effect** saving throws against effects that induce strong emotions. 

A dose lasts for **four hours**, though it affects a user only once every 24 hours.
