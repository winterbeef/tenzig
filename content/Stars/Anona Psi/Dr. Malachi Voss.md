A brilliant but morally dubious scientist, Dr. Voss resents [[Octavius Cromwell]]'s control over the pretech vault. Voss desires access to the advanced technologies for his own experiments and is willing to go to great lengths to achieve his goals.

Currently visiting [[Clova 7]] by offering [[Octavius Cromwell]] first dibs on some "...priceless Pretech artifacts, retrieved at my great cost and peril...".  In reality, some authentic but -- mostly -- unexciting curios bought from a "dealer in authentic relics" on [[Rafquat]].
