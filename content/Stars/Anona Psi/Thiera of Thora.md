[[Thora]] is a planet characterized by a rigid feudal society.  The strictly-observed social strata are known as the Thiera. The societal structure is deeply stratified, with each Thiera occupying a specific social tier, from [[The Dynasteia of Thora]] (dee-nas-TEE-ah) to the lowest stratum *Spilios*, the Filth. Thora's culture is marked by strict adherence to tradition, ceremonial rituals, and a conservative approach to governance.

Citizens are assigned Thiera at birth, a function of the parent's Thiera and mandatory gene testing. It is rare, but a Thoran may be assigned to a Thiera different from their parents'.

The ruler of [[Thora]], [[Exalted Autarch Damocles Kallistrate]], leads with an iron grip, maintaining the status quo through a combination of political acumen and military strength. The ruler's legitimacy is derived from a glorious bloodline, instilling a sense of divine right among the Thiera.

Agriculture is extremely important to Thora, as its main export to many other worlds. The Farmers (Thiera: Georgos -- jor-GOS) could have collective power but the political organization is such to keep them unorganized.  Regional governance is by lesser Dynasteia and attendant Thesauria.
