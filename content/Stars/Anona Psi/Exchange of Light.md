# History

Prior to the Scream, The Exchange was the backbone of the far trader banking and communication network.  The Exchange itself promoted diplomatic harmony and economic development as an article of faith, and were involved in all manner of projects to improve the wealth of its consular worlds.

The Silence grievously wounded the Exchange. Each planetary Consulate was cut off from the others, left to the resources of the local Exchange members.
