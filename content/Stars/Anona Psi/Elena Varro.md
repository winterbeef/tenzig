#game/npc on [[Thora]], Ergasia (Worker)

![[elena varro.png|300]]

A skilled technician and manager at a agricultural manufactory.  Irreplaceable, in fact.

Also secretly the leader of [[The Whispered Promise]], a clandestine movement advocating for social reform. Aims to break free from the oppressive social structure and introduce progressive ideals.

Organizes peaceful protests, disseminates subversive literature, and recruits sympathizers.

Elena is extraordinarily compassionate and cannot refuse an appeal from the disadvantaged and downtrodden.  She recognizes that her idealism may get her in trouble, but she cannot, and will not betray her moral code.

She abhors violence but other voices in the movement are not as restrained.
