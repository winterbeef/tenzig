#game/npc on [[Thora]], Thesauria

![[aneas.jpg|300]]

Scholar and Historian, Heads [[The Golden Quill Society]].

- *Motivation:* Seeks the forgotten truths about Thora's past, no matter the displeasure of the rulers and social order.
- *Methods:* Conducts field research, visiting sites of archeological interest.  Writes contrarian literature that often challenges accepted history.  Encourages intellectual discourse, organizing debates and symposia.

Interested in exploring the [[Ruins of Myristos]].
