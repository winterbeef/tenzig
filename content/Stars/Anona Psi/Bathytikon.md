#game/alien 

![[bathytikon.jpeg]]

Originally native to the [[Argera]] system. A species at home in the great pressures of gas giants, and in their massive pressurized suits. 

Disinterested with the humans plight or success.  They seek the great gas giants in this sector for colonization, leaving mostly other species unbothered.

They often trade for industrial quantities of metals, for unknown reasons.
