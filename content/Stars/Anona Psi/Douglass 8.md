#game/researchbase on [[Thora]]

# Background

Five years ago, the normally insular Thorans granted a dig rights to the [[Royal Society for Advanced Anthropological Studies]] of [[Panidoulo]] when the lead researchers suggested that they were researching some leads that could "prove" the legitimacy of [[The Dynasteia of Thora]].  The society is excavating in a deep mine rumored to be pre-Scream.

The proof is rumored to be contained in some ancient religious artifacts rumored to be buried deep under this dig site.

The society is in fact a group of freelance scientists hired by go-between fixers on behalf of [[The Perimeter Agency - Corps VII]] -- who are investigating rumors of the first *Nihil Ultra* of the Corps: *Thou shalt not make tools of humankind.* 

The researchers are led by the monomaniacal [[Doctor Dmitri Ortov]]. 
# Situation

Recently, the researchers at Douglass 8 have uncovered structures and artifacts with the dawning realization that they do not align with known Thoran history. This has led them to a startling conclusion: [[Douglass 8]] is not a site of human Thoran history, but rather the ruins of a long-dead alien race.

This alien race, known as the [[Xalvorians]], were masters of bioengineering and used carefully-tailored viruses to enslave other species. The site that [[Doctor Dmitri Ortov]] and his team are exploring is, in fact, an ancient bioweapons lab filled with dangerous and potent biological agents designed to control and manipulate sentient beings.

One of these may be the [[Comealong Virus]].

**Artifact Discovery:** The team discovers a sealed chamber containing ancient [[Xalvorians]] technology and virus samples. Initial analysis reveals their horrifying purpose.

**Moral Dilemma:** [[Doctor Dmitri Ortov]], driven by his obsession, sees the potential of these bioweapons for advancing human understanding and power, despite the obvious ethical and existential risks.

**Perimeter Agency:** The Perimeter Agency, learning of these discoveries, sends a team to either secure or destroy the bioweapons to prevent them from falling into the wrong hands.
# Events and Complications

1. **Containment Breach:** One of the ancient viruses is accidentally released within [[Douglass 8]], causing chaos and forcing the players to contain the outbreak and find a cure.
   
2. **Xalvorian Secrets:** The deeper the players delve into the ruins, the more they uncover about the [[Xalvorians]]'s advanced technology and the true extent of their control over other species.

3. **Reawakening:** The ruins house an ancient [[Xalvorians]] AI that awakens and attempts to continue its creators' work, posing a new threat to both the research team and the players.
