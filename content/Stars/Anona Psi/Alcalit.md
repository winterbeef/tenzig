Alcalit has retained much of its pre-tech facilities and expertise in robotics, VI/AI engineering and cybernetics.  Expert systems, VIs and AIs are roughly 50% the price listed in the books (pp 197).  Cybernetics are roughly 75%.

A full-body "beast-cyber" will cost ($100k), but there are various levels of commitment.  There are pheromone emitters ($10k) , gills ($10k), prehensile tails, and lots of ears ($7k), especially those of the BEAST a native herbivore with exceptional hearing.

Worship of Enki figures prominently.  The [[Catechist of the Silicon]] is a pervasive faith, and on [[Alcalit]] has formed a synectric faith with the more ancient **Akkadianism**.

**Enki, the God of Wisdom and Artificial Intelligence**: Enki is the wise and cunning god of wisdom, knowledge, and magic. In the society of [[Alcalit]], he is also revered as the patron deity of artificial intelligence and cybernetic augmentation. Enki's temples house repositories of knowledge and serve as centers for AI research and development.

The demagouge [[Serenity Sofia Fernandes]] is popular, preaching for harmony among sentients -- Humans, VIs and AIs.
