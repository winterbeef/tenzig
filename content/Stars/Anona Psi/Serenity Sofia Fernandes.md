The demagouge [[Serenity Sofia Fernandes]] is popular on [[Alcalit]], preaching for harmony among sentients -- Humans, VIs and AIs. 

She is a high-ranking member of the  [[Catechist of the Silicon]] and influential in Alcalitian politics.
