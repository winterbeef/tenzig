[[Factions in Anona Psi MOC]]

A #game/faction on [[Thora]].

A "voluntary association" of Thorans that serve as role models and shepherds for the greater number of citizenry. They have the responsibility of certifying the legitimacy of the [[Exalted Autarch Damocles Kallistrate]].

In fact they are more of a secret police force composed of neighbors spying on neighbors, family betraying family, in service of the chance at greater social standing.
