[[Factions in Anona Psi MOC]]

The ***Liberationists*** of [[Ninon]],  opposing both the Ninonian Loyalists ([[Council of Regents of Ninon]]) and the  Secretly supported and funded by [[The Glorious Path]].


# Stats
## Tags

**Deep Rooted**
This faction has been part of a world's life for time out of mind. Most natives can hardly imagine the world without this faction's presence, and the traditional prerogatives and dignities of the group are instinctively respected.

Effect: This faction can roll one additional `dice: d10` when defending against attacks on assets on their homeworld. If the faction ever changes homeworlds, this tag is lost.

# Goal

**Invincible Valor** 
Destroy a Force asset with a mini- mum purchase rating higher than your faction’s Force rating. Thus, if your Force is 3, you need to destroy a unit that requires Force 4 or higher to purchase. Difficulty 2.
