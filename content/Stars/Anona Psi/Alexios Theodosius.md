Registry Officer/Customs Control  (Bureaucrat/*Thesauria*)
Works with [[Eirene Nikophoros]]

Must register ships, and individuals leaving the Port.  Supervises cargo inspection, enforces trade compliance.

Reserved, taciturn, standoffish.  Speaks volumes when reading from his script, which he does in a monotone, uncaring if anyone hears.  Just doing his job, resigned to this existence.


> Welcome to [[Porta Intacta]]. This is standard protocol. Please register any complaints with the *Customs Control Head Office*, located in the *Ministry of Registered Agencies* in [[Hierápolis]] *Government Center, 23g//b*. 
> 
> We need to know the full contents of your vessel. Do you have any hazardous materials on board, such as antimatter, unshielded fusion cores, or any form of nanotech that hasn't been declared and verified? We also need to confirm whether you are transporting any biological specimens, living or otherwise, including but not limited to: xenomorphs, genetically modified organisms, or undocumented extraterrestrial flora. Please declare any high-energy weapons, military-grade tech, or AI systems with autonomous decision-making capabilities.
> 
> Additionally, we need to verify if you're carrying any cultural artifacts from restricted zones, particularly from planets under the *Intergalactic Preservation Act*. Are there any perishables that require quarantine clearance, such as alien livestock or produce from uncharted worlds? Finally, confirm your cargo of commercial goods, including quantities and intended destinations, and if you're transporting any passengers, their documentation and travel permits need to be provided. Non-compliance will result in immediate impoundment of your ship and all contents therein. Thank you for your cooperation.
