[[Factions in Anona Psi MOC]]
[[Faction Play in Anona Psi]]


- *Nature:* Secret society working for societal change.
- *Members:* Diverse mix of individuals from various tiers, united by a common desire for reform.
- *Goals:* Subtly challenge the existing order and lay the groundwork for a more inclusive society.

- Led by [[Elena Varro]], compassionate, pacifistic.
- [[Theodorus Romanos]], tall and wiry, with scarred hands and arms.  Fervid and combative.
- Aligned with [[Sophia Nikophoros]], progressivist clergy.
- Aligned with [[Andronikos Ioannes]], author and activist

Opposed by [[The Dynasteia of Thora]] and [[Citizens of Faultless Virtue]].
