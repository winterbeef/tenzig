#game/place on [[Thora]]

![[Palace of Unbroken Vigilance.png]]

The [[Palace of Unbroken Vigilance]] has stood for centuries, its foundations laid by the first Autarch, Leonidas Palaiologos. Legend has it that the celestial alignment at the time of its construction was deemed auspicious, marking the birth of a golden era for [[Thora]]. Each successive Autarch has left their mark on the palace, expanding its halls and adding to its grandeur.

A significant event in its history was the Celestial Accord, a pact signed in the Throne Room between the ruling Autarch and representatives of the Thiera. The accord established the delicate balance of power, preserving the harmony of Thora's society.
