The capital city of [[Ninon]]. The seat of the [[Council of Regents of Ninon]].

The beleaguered capital stands as a stark contrast between its once-promising beauty and the grim realities of its current state, overshadowed by the corrupt [[Council of Regents of Ninon]]. Once a symbol of prosperity, the city is now marred by neglect and decay under the iron grip of this ruling elite.

The architectural marvels and colonial-era buildings that once gleamed are now crumbling, their facades stained and unkempt, reflecting the neglect that has seeped into every corner of the city. Despite the natural beauty of the verdant hills and Azul Bay, a pall of despair hangs over Nova Alvorada as the council's mismanagement and corruption stifles its growth and prosperity.

The city itself is nestled in a broad valley, surrounded by a series of low, rolling hills that are lush with verdant forests. These hills serve as a green belt around the urban sprawl, offering not only scenic beauty but also hiking and recreational opportunities for the residents.

To the south of Nova Alvorada, the terrain gently slopes towards the majestic Azul Bay, whose calm, turquoise waters contrast with the vibrant greens of the hills. The bay is a natural harbor and has historically been a crucial part of the city’s economy, although its usage has declined under the current mismanagement. Along the coastline, sandy beaches stretch intermittently, dotted with rocky outcrops and cliffs that offer stunning views of the ocean.

To the north of the city, the roads cut through hills and through the mountain range about 25 kM out.
