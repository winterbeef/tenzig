# Anona Psi Travelogue

## Spacers Superstitions

- Step onto a ship with your right foot first
- Observe 
- Bad luck to throw out a "Little Trees" starship freshener
- Always buy a new one before a drill
- Cats (xenocats too) are lucky
- Shaving onboard is unlucky
- Never pass the salt directly.  Put it down, let the other pick it up.
- Throw some bread crust in the trash when dumping it.  Distracts the gremlins that otherwise causse problems on the ship
- Beware the Void Kraken!  A metaspace creature of horrible visage! Those who see it are doomed to never return. Rumors abound:
	- Vinegar repels it
	- Vinegar attracts it
	- Have your ship blessed by a priest on it's christening day

## Well-known Worlds and Other Points of Interest 

- [[Thora]]: An insular and xenophobic world, ruled by unwavering observance to "The Old Ways".  Founded in 2560, after the jump gate was brought to this sector.
- [[Clova 7]] Deepspace playground of an eccentric oligarch, [[Octavius Cromwell]].
- [[Ortasto]]: A world of free merchant traders, and the mercenary companies they hire.
- [[Rafquat]] a pilgrimage site for the [[Catechist of the Silicon]], rumored to have Pretech artifacts among the holy relics.
- [[Ninon]], formerly a world of great warlords who seemed destined to rule this sector; now a world wracked by revolution.

## Religions

### The Most Holy [[Catechist of the Silicon]]

The mysteries of existence will only be revealed when humans, VIs and AI are seen as equals. 

### Avestani

The new Zoroasters. A dualistic cosmology; there is an absolute good (Ahura) and absolute evil (Angra).  

There is not so much a single god as a single source of Good from which we are born. Our acts in this world are judged.

The words and example of a relatively recent prophet, esteeming him or her as the final word on the will of Ahura.
- Leadership: Council. A group of the oldest and most revered clergy determine the course of the faith.
