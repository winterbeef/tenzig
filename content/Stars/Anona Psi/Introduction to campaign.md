The **Sector** is the the entirety of the setting, all the stars and planets, etc., I'm willing to create.
A hex represents a **System**; a star and its attendant planets, asteroid belts, refueling stations, etc.
The coordinates are a  four digit number. The first two are the *column*. The last two are the *row*.

# Party Options

Here are some options for what the characters or the party might be.  Just here for inspiration and discussion.  This has no game mechanical effect; just creating background and perhaps informing the likely adventures and challenges that may be presented.

## Merchants

Might be members of the [[Trade Pact]]. Or perhaps the [[Exchange of Light]].

You seek a foothold for your guild on [[Thora]], an insular world. Or you are Free Mercants looking to make some profit on the markets, white, gray or black.

## Mercenaries

Might have arrived from [[Ortasto]], a world of mercenaries. 

You may be providing security for a merchant seeking to buy relics from [[Rafquat]], or for the [[The Perimeter Agency - Corps VII]], monitoring pretech discoveries.

You may have been hired by legitimate or illegitimate clients for a significant transaction.

## Deniable Intelligence Assets

The players are former intelligence assets or agents, disavowed by their employer. You might be working for [[Ninon]] as one of several groups vie for control in that world. Or in [[Thora]], an insular and caste-bound world. Or maybe for [[The Glorious Path]], a revolutionary movement seeking to overthrow tyrannical governments.

## Bounty Hunters

A bounty posted on [[MercNet]] on [[Xandro Simonides]], the captain of the [[Casual Dance]], a bounty that he insists is nothing but an unjust vendetta for a honest deal gone bad.

## Salvagers

There's Titanium, Neodymium, Yttrium, Hexacore, Nanofiber, Plasteel, Keratan, Heptanium, QiN, all just floating in the dark, radiation-filled vacuum of space, waiting to be claimed! There's salvage 

## Explorers

The players have joined together as an expedition crew searching for lost alien technology. They’ve heard rumors of ancient artifacts and forgotten temples on the planet they've just arrived at, and each member has their own reasons for being here, whether it’s for academic discovery, profit, or personal fulfillment.

Rumors that on [[Thora]], the [[Wreck of the Taiyang]], a pretech ship, have been found is worth investigation. Who knows what pretech wonders may still lie undiscovered within?

The [[Ruins of Myristos]] remain unexplored, as its remote location remains lethal, and its secrets remain frustratingly out of reach.

## Criminals

Smugglers, grifters, killers for hire. You have a target -- a well-protected black marketeer kingpin whose rivals have identified a potential ambush opportunity, either to threaten, assault or kill. A black marketeer kingpin hires you to threaten, assault or kill a rival.

Rumors that the playground of the oligarch [[Octavius Cromwell]], the deep space station [[Clova 7]], houses priceless treasures intrigues you. Perhaps for study, perhaps for liberation.

## Escaped Prisoners

Might be from [[Euryna]], a prison planet.  Or any other world where someone wanted you imprisoned.

## Refugees

Might be from [[Sudhita Kam]], [[Ninon]], [[Euryna]], [[Iara]].  Might be fleeing from a colony slow ship that never made it to its destination, now decaying in the void.


# Sector, Common Knowledge

## Worlds

### [[Thora]]
![[thora.jpeg|300]] ![[farming on thora.jpg|300]]
[[Thora]] ([[Heliomachos]]) an insular an unwelcoming world of strict heirarchies and an entrenched ruling class. No wonder a thriving black market exists, with merchants eager to open the world to new trade.  Still, abundant agicultural harvest.


### [[Clyte]]
![[clyte.jpeg|300]] ![[clyte patagonian desert.jpeg]]

The world [[Clyte]] ([[Paruce XVIII]]) is a primitive backwater world. Clyte was to be the terraformed jewel in the crown of the Mandate, but alas, the [[Sector History|Scream]] ruined that.

### [[Ortasto]]

![[ortasto.jpg|300]]

![[ortasto docks.jpg]]

[[Ortasto]] ([[Pharoslux]]) is a harsh world on which to make a living. Despite the unforgiving conditions, situated at the nexus of several stable spike drill courses, its denizens have thrived.  A world of mercenaries and merchants.


### [[Skakis]]

![[skakis.jpg|300]]
[[Skakis]] ([[Pharoslux]]) is a cold world where the inhabitants have resorted to extreme bio-engineering to survive.


### [[Rafquat]]

![[rafquat.jpg|300]]

![[rafquat pilgrims and towers.jpg|400]]

[[Rafquat]] ([[Stratophanes]]) never fully recovered from the Scream. Nonetheless, it benefits from being an attraction for the millions of pilgrims that visit. The world attracts those interested in pretech, as an abundance of artifacts have been found here.  The [[The Perimeter Agency - Corps VII]] has recently dispatched a task force to monitor pretech discoveries.


### [[Euryna]]

![[Euryna.jpg|300]]

![[euryna - prison.jpg|400]]


[[Euryna]] ([[Stratophanes]]) is a miserable world, with no reason to visit. The small population are mostly prisoners, banished and forgotten.

### [[Ninon]]

![[ninon planet.jpg|300]]

![[ninon on fire.jpg|400]]

[[Ninon]] ([[Arcio]]) is a world of former glory, now wracked by internal strife, and that pot stirred by the revolutionary guard of [[The Glorious Path]].

## Factions and Religions and Other Groups

[[The Perimeter Agency - Corps VII]] proclaims itself  "...the last bulwark humanity has against the depredations of chaos and oblivion..." Pre-scream they opposed maltech -- unjustifiable gengineering, unbraked AI research, planet-snuffing weapons, etc.

[[The Glorious Path]] is the People's Movement spreading across the sector to the alarm of entrenched rulers. Their goal is the overthrow of undemocratic planetary governments.

[[Trade Pact]] an informal mutual aid society of free merchants.

[[Royal Society for Advanced Anthropological Studies]], an academic and research organization with a mission to catalog and preserve important discoveries.  Backed by wealthy and mostly anonymous donors.

[[Catechist of the Silicon]] is a powerful church devoted to understanding, accord, and harmony between organic and artificial sentients. [[Rafquat]] is said to be holy to the church.

[[Council of Regents of Ninon]] is the interim ruling body of [[Ninon]], since the disappearance of the [[Divine Emperor of Ninon]], two years ago

[[Valdez Combine]] a resource extraction concern "...committed to responsible stewardship..."

The [[Korolev Dispatch]], a totally 100% legitimate shipping concern renowned for its efficient cargo haulers and ability to ship to dangerous zones.
