# Opening Scenes in Anona Psi

The [[Casual Dance]] is a free merchant captained by [[Xandro Simonides]], a well-travelled free trader.  The Dance drills into the edge of the [[Heliomachos]] system, floating on the dark, listening to system chatter and sifting through the news cached in the comm bouy network.

A Thoran patrol boat -- *[[The Euthymius]]* --  idles nearby, monitoring system arrivals.  The boat does not deign to engage in any comms, but makes a show of actively pinging the arrival region.

## Danger Heist

Thievery, exfiltration

The characters are deep inside a series of vaults on [[Lassell 7]], alarms blaring around them as they scramble to bypass the final security measures. The prize -- a device of immense value or great danger -- sits within a sealed chamber ahead, but the station's security forces are closing in fast.  Just as they think they’ve cracked the last code, the VI voice announces, "Vault seal in 60 seconds." They have to decide: grab the loot and escape, or leave empty-handed and survive.


## The Race to the Wreck

Combat, environmental hazards, scavenging

The characters are plummeting from low orbit toward the [[Wreck of the Taiyang]], where a valuable starship wreck lies half-buried in a toxic swamp. As they break through the clouds, they see rival scavenger crews already swarming over the wreckage, armed and ready to kill for the prize. Their transport slams into the ground with a shuddering impact, scattering the wildlife and sending up plumes of swamp water. The race is on to secure the wreck’s valuable cargo before the others do.

## Desperate Escape

Escaping pusuit, environmental hazards, data acquisition

The characters burst out of a research lab on fire, gasping for air as the facility behind them explodes in a massive fireball. They've just stolen classified research data from [[Douglass 8]], but now the base's automated defenses are hunting them down. The jungle is thick and disorienting, and they can hear the whir of drones closing in. They have only moments to decide their next move: take a hidden path they spotted earlier or risk the direct route to their extraction point.

## Crash Landing

Escaping pusuit, environmental hazards, bandits and deadly fauna.  The [[Casual Dance]] has been shot out of the sky by salvagers "defending their legitimate claim to salvage" -- the [[Ruins of Myristos]].

If the [[The Golden Quill Society]] has employed the PCs to investigate the ruins, then it may be [[Professor Aeneas Xanthopoulos]]

The PCs may wake upside-down, strapped into their jump seats while the glitchy fire supression system sprays suppressant powder ineffectively.  They should act quickly.

Hold your breath for 5 rounds modified by CON.  Thereafter, Physical save every round or take 1 point from smoke inhalation.

PCs have their equipped items and backpacks at the ready, but salvaging other items from the shuttle will require either fighting the fire or braving the smoke.  Skill checks to retrieve equipment.
