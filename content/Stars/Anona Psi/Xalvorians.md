This long-extinct, alien race, known as the [[Xalvorians]], were masters of bioengineering and used carefully-tailored viruses to enslave other species. 

[[Douglass 8]] may be the ruins of one of their labs.

Some [[Szaichib]] may suspect these gene masters enslaved their race.
