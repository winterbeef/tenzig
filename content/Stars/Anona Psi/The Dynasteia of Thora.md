# Lore

Established in 2700, some 35 years after the Scream.  The clergy of the new faith that proclaimed the rise of Thora promoted the [[Thiera of Thora]], as a way towards the fulfillment of a destiny.

Legend has it that the first Lords of Thora were granted dominion and blessed by the Great Gods of [[Argera]]:

The Flamebringer in the ancient myths of the [[Xalvorians]] was revered as the god of light, knowledge, and leadership. According to legend, he descended from the heavens in a chariot of fire to unite the warring tribes of Thora. He is depicted as a majestic figure holding the [[Staff of Providence]], symbolizing his divine right to rule and bring enlightenment to his people.

## Tags

**Deep Rooted**
This faction has been part of a world’s life for time out of mind. Most natives can hardly imagine the world without this faction’s presence, and the traditional prerogatives and dignities of the group are instinctively respected.

Effect: This faction can roll one additional `dice: d10` when defending against attacks on assets on their homeworld. If the faction ever changes homeworlds, this tag is lost.
