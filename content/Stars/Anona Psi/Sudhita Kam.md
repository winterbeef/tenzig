The thick atmosphere makes it hard to see, but infrared passes through, making it uncomfortably warm.  Savage monsoons rack the surface seasonally for several months the year (25%).

Dead petrified forests litter the surface, the mute artifacts left over from the terraforming engines failure after the Scream.

Buildings have airlocks and have pitched roofs to shed the frequent rainstorms.

The worldwide Logistical Asset Management System (LAMS) strictly enforces quotas, curfews, travel, population and all other aspects of life on the planet.

This world is being systematically contained by an outside power. Some ancient autonomous defense grid, robot law enforcement, alien artifact, or other force is preventing the locals from developing certain technology, or using certain devices, or perhaps from developing interstellar flight.
