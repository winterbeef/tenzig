#game/planet in the [[Arcio]] system

[[Admiral Mateus Pereira]] and [[Countess Isadora Costa]] have fled here for [[Clova 7]]

Currently ruled by the [[Council of Regents of Ninon]], from the capital of [[Nova Alvorada]], whose rule is opposed by revolutionaries -- the [[Peoples Justice Movement]] -- rumored to be backed by [[The Glorious Path]].

Formally, **The Imperium of Ninon**, the government has been assumed by the [[Council of Regents of Ninon]] since the disappearance of the [[Divine Emperor of Ninon]]. 

The Hero of the People, [[Great Admiral Pedro Carvalho]], (*Leão*) is the unassuming military genius that has led Ninon in keeping peace in this corner of the sector for years. 

# Factions, Major and Minor

- The ***Loyalists***; those who preserve fealty to the [[Divine Emperor of Ninon]]
- The self-named ***Progressivists***; those who back the [[Council of Regents of Ninon]] as the legitimate governors of Ninon.
- The ***Liberationists***; represented by the [[Peoples Justice Movement]]. Secretly supported and funded by [[The Glorious Path]].
