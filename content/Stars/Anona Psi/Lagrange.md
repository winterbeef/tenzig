[[Factions in Anona Psi MOC]]

![[Lagrange.jpeg]]

It is a rumour only.  A fable.  A story of a clandestine organization shrouded in mystery, known only to a select few who have encountered its enigmatic agents or benefited from its anonymous aid. Led by a small cabal of highly influential individuals, the leadership operates in the shadows, pulling strings behind the scenes to shape the course of human history.

They understand the importance of maintaining their invisibility, knowing that exposure could jeopardize their ability to enact change from the shadows.

Whether it's funding groundbreaking research, orchestrating strategic alliances, or toppling oppressive regimes, the interventions are carefully calculated to maximize their impact while minimizing their traceability.

To those who have encountered their benevolence, [[Lagrange]] is whispered about in hushed tones, spoken of in awe and reverence. They are the unseen hand guiding humanity towards a brighter tomorrow, their influence shaping the course of history in ways both subtle and profound. But to the outside world, they remain a mystery, their true intentions and motivations known only to those who have earned their trust.

## Tags

**Secretive**
This faction is marked by elaborate protocols of secrecy and misdirection. It may be split up into numerous semi-autonomous cells, or consist largely of trained espionage professionals. Finding the assets of such a faction can often be more difficult than destroying them. 

**Effect**
All assets purchased by this faction automatically begin Stealthed. See the list of Cunning assets for details on Stealth.
