#game/npc on [[Thora]], Strategia

> [!quote] Pax Per Ordinem
> Peace Through Order
> 


![[Commander Kyrios Drakon, Military Enforcer.png]]


Believes in a militarized society to maintain order and control.

Suppresses dissent within the military, enforces loyalty through fear, and squashes any hint of rebellion.

Feels that [[Exalted Autarch Damocles Kallistrate]] should give the military (him) freer reign over the civilian populace, keeping "Peace Through Order".

Hated by his staff and own troops; seen as ruthlessly ambitious and cruel.
