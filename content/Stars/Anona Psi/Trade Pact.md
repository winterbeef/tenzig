An informal network of traders and merchants who recognize the benefits of cooperation in some aspects of their enterprise.

- A trader will provide aid to another in the case of ship malfunction, pirate attack or medical emergency.
- Traders will share info about hazards, natural and artificial, to the degree that it does not interfere with their own profit.
- Traders often pool resources -- credit and haulage -- to make larger deals possible.
- Dispute resolution.  An informal council may be convened and though the judgments are non-binding they are usually grudgingly accepted as part of good standing in the community.


Leena "The Broker" Thorne
Renowned for her negotiation skills and ability to secure the best deals, Leena often mediates disputes within the **Dispute Resolution Council**.

Jackson "Monkey" Duval
An expert mechanic and engineer, Jackson teaches traders vital repair and maintenance skills, and helps direct the maintenance and repair of ships.
