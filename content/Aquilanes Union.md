The coreward part of the sector.
Major systems are [[Perope]] (0404) -- hosting the planet [[Tabet]] -- and [[Rado]] (0303) -- hosting [[Lycos]]. Nearby is the super-massive, super-violent blackhole [[MCXT Scylla]] (0304).

Far off is [[Campera]] (0206), home to some of the finest shipyards in the sector.

The AqU formed when the first worlds began recovering from the Scream, and banded together to better confront the barbarism and violence that threatened all civilized societies.

Many AqU member worlds would like to see the [[Council of Speakers]] to take a more assertive approach in guiding the systems of the Tenzig sector towards a more prosperous future.
