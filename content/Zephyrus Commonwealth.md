The name belies the nature of the commonwealth. In truth, the ZC is a systems-spanning feudalist polity. The princes of member worlds answer to the [[High Guardian of the Commonwealth]], while exercising a great deal of sovereignty over their own worlds.

[[Cantho]], in the [[Savris]] system (0402), wields great influence.  The Central Planning Council actually has succeeded in providing for all the needs of its citizens.  Nonetheless, there are always malcontents, if you know where to look.

[[Shi Vashree]], in the [[Mussad]] system (0501), is unwelcoming. The lack of atmosphere has made life in the bubblehabs harsh, and unforgiving.

Meanhwile, [[Arnorun]] of the [[Kos]] system (0603) is a primitive idyll, of simple inhabitants, living peaceful lives among the alien ruins that dot the world.
