The [[Heirs of Carderas]] are a pirate gang that make their home among the belt.  

Ancient ruins of unknown origin have been found among the belt, though the presence of the pirates has made further exploration difficult.

The ruins creators are fabled to either be some long-lost advanced artificial life, or now extinct alien species.  Older spacers speak of the [[Inuar]] in hushed whispers.
